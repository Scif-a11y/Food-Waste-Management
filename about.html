About Us

At Anna, we are dedicated to tackling food waste and promoting sustainable practices in our communities. Our mission is to educate individuals and organizations about the importance of minimizing waste, redistributing surplus food, and composting organic materials.

Our Mission

We aim to create a world where food is valued, and waste is minimized. Through our programs and initiatives, we strive to:

  • Raise awareness about food waste issues.
  • Implement effective waste reduction strategies.
  • Support local food recovery efforts.

Impact of Food Waste

Food waste is a major global issue, contributing to climate change and resource depletion. Here are some alarming statistics:

  • Approximately one-third of all food produced globally is wasted each year.
  • Food waste generates around 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions.
  • In the U.S. alone, food waste is estimated to cost approximately $218 billion annually.
